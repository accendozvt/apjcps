About APJCPS

Where Ambition Meets Opportunity

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam College of Professional Studies (APJCPS) is a new-generation institution nestled in Iritty, Kannur, Kerala - established under the Rural Educational Trust and affiliated to Kannur University.

We offer four industry-aligned undergraduate programs, with a standout distinction: we are home to Kerala's first regular degree in Aviation & Airport Management, delivered in partnership with Kannur International Airport. Our campus blends modern infrastructure with a learning culture that emphasises skill, character, and career readiness from day one.

Our Degree Programs

Four industry-aligned undergraduate courses - each designed to open doors to the careers of tomorrow

Kerala's First

BBA - Aviation & Airport Management

The only regular aviation degree in Kerala, delivered in live collaboration with Kannur International Airport. Students train on real airport operations, airline systems, and passenger services - graduating job-ready for one of the world's fastest-growing industries.

  • Airport operations & ground handling
  • Airline ticketing & reservation systems (Amadeus/Galileo)
  • Cargo & logistics management
  • Cabin crew & passenger service training
  • Live internship at Kannur International Airport
Degree or Honours - 3 or 4 Years Bachelor's Degree 100% Placement Assistance

BCA - Computer Applications with AI

A future-forward technology degree that blends core computer science fundamentals with hands-on training in Artificial Intelligence, machine learning, and full-stack development. The integrated AI add-on ensures graduates are ready for roles in tech, data, and product companies.

  • Programming: Python, Java, C++, web technologies
  • AI & Machine Learning add-on course
  • Database management & cloud computing
  • Cybersecurity fundamentals
  • Industry live projects & internship
Degree or Honours - 3 or 4 Years Bachelor's Degree AI Add-on Certified

BBA - Business Administration with Logistics & Supply Chain

A comprehensive management degree that builds business acumen alongside logistics and supply chain expertise - one of the most in-demand skill sets in e-commerce, retail, and manufacturing. Students emerge as confident managers and entrepreneurs.

  • Marketing, HR & financial management
  • Supply chain & inventory management
  • Logistics operations & warehousing
  • Entrepreneurship & business planning
  • Industry visits & management internship
Degree or Honours - 3 or 4 Years Bachelor's Degree Logistics Add-on

B.Com - Finance with Logistics & Supply Chain

A rigorous commerce and finance degree enhanced with a logistics add-on, preparing students for careers in banking, auditing, taxation, financial planning, and the ever-expanding logistics sector. Ideal for students targeting CA, MBA, or direct employment.

  • Accounting, taxation & auditing
  • Corporate finance & investment basics
  • GST, income tax & Tally ERP training
  • Logistics & supply chain management add-on
  • CA/MBA foundation pathway guidance
Degree or Honours - 3 or 4 Years Bachelor's Degree Logistics Add-on

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you take the next step

APJCPS currently offers four undergraduate degree programs: BBA in Aviation & Airport Management, BCA in Computer Applications with AI, BBA in Business Administration with Logistics & Supply Chain, and B.Com in Finance with Logistics & Supply Chain. Each program is 3 or 4 years under Kannur University's FYUGP framework and comes with a valuable industry add-on certification.

Our BBA in Aviation & Airport Management is the only regular undergraduate aviation degree in Kerala. Students receive live training at Kannur International Airport, covering airport operations, ground handling, airline ticketing (Amadeus/Galileo systems), cargo management, and cabin crew service. This real-world exposure sets our graduates apart in the competitive aviation job market.

All programs follow Kannur University's Four-Year Undergraduate Programme (FYUGP). Students may exit with a certificate after 1 year, a diploma after 2 years, a Bachelor's degree after 3 years, or an Honours/Honours with Research degree after 4 years - giving you flexibility to choose your academic journey.

Admission is open to students who have completed their Plus Two (10+2) or equivalent from any recognised board. For BCA, a background in Mathematics or Computer Science is preferred but not mandatory. Admissions follow Kannur University guidelines and the Kerala CAP (Centralised Admission Process). Contact our admissions office for stream-specific requirements.

Yes - we offer 100% Placement Assistance through our dedicated placement cell. This includes resume building workshops, mock interview sessions, aptitude training, campus recruitment drives, and direct industry referrals. Our network of employer partners spans aviation, IT, BFSI, logistics, and retail sectors across India and the Gulf region.

Each program includes a structured add-on certification: BCA students receive an Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning certification, while BBA and B.Com students receive a Logistics & Supply Chain Management certification. These are in addition to the core degree and significantly improve employability and salary prospects for our graduates.

APJCPS is located at Vilamana, Iritty, Kannur, Kerala. The campus is well-connected by road and is accessible from Iritty town. The nearest railway station is Kannur (approximately 40 km). For detailed directions or transport guidance, please contact us at +91 7909 1000 81.

Hostel facilities are available nearby and can be arranged with assistance from the college administration. We help students find suitable, safe, and affordable accommodation close to campus. Please reach out to the admissions office for the latest availability and details at apjcollegeps@gmail.com or +91 7909 1000 81.
